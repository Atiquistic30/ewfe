The Legacy

The Music That
Moved Generations

Al McKay is one of the founding fathers of Earth, Wind & Fire — the legendary Chicago-born ensemble that defined the sound of an era. As the band's lead guitarist and principal co-writer, McKay shaped anthems that transcended genre: September, Let's Groove, Boogie Wonderland, Saturday Nite, Fantasy — the songs a generation grew up loving.

The Earth, Wind & Fire Experience by Al McKay is not a tribute act. It is the authentic continuation of that musical legacy — a world-class ensemble of musicians who have toured alongside Al McKay across Europe, the Americas, and beyond, delivering every note with the precision and soul of the original recordings.

On the night of 16 August 2026, under the extraordinary skies of Paestum's ancient amphitheater, that legacy returns to Italy for a concert that will be remembered long after the last note fades.

FAQ

Everything You
Need to Know.

Arena dei Templi is located within the archaeological park of Paestum, along Via Magna Graecia, in the province of Salerno (SA), Campania. It is approximately 90km south of Naples and 30km south of Salerno. The nearest railway station is Paestum (Trenitalia Regionale). Full address: Via Magna Graecia, 84047 Capaccio Paestum (SA), Italy.
The show will proceed in all but the most severe weather conditions. August in Paestum is historically warm and dry. In the event of cancellation due to extreme weather, all ticket holders will be notified immediately and offered a full refund. We recommend bringing a light layer as evenings near the sea can cool down after midnight.
Yes, parking is available in and around the Paestum archaeological park. Additional overflow parking is signposted along Via Magna Graecia. We recommend arriving early — gates open at 20:00 — as parking fills quickly for major events. Shuttle services from Paestum train station may be available; check the event updates closer to the date.
Compact cameras and smartphones are welcome. Professional DSLR/mirrorless cameras with detachable lenses and video recording equipment are not permitted without prior press accreditation. Recording of the performance for personal, non-commercial use is at the discretion of venue staff on the night.
The event is all-ages. Children under 12 may attend free of charge when accompanied by a ticket-holding adult (maximum one child per adult). As this is an evening outdoor concert, we recommend the event for ages 5 and above. Please note the show does not end before 23:00.
The Earth, Wind & Fire Experience by Al McKay is led by Al McKay himself — the original co-founder, guitarist, and principal co-writer of Earth, Wind & Fire. This is not a tribute act or impersonator show. The band features seasoned professionals who have toured with Al McKay for years, performing the music exactly as he and his co-writers intended it. When you hear September or Let's Groove here, you are hearing it performed by the people who helped create it.
